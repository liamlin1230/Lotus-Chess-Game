To start game, provide correct run arguments. 
Arguments must consist of either finch or mouse first, followed by the names of the players.
Needs to be at least 2 players and at most 6 players.

R player starts first by clicking a button from their pile. 
If pop box appears, click which track to go.

First player to get all of their pieces of the board wins (10 pieces for 2-3 players and 6 for 4-6 players).
A popup box will appear announcing the winner.
